Plan Your Visit

WildHaven Park offers an unforgettable experience. Here's everything you need to know to plan a perfect visit, from travel tips to available packages.

Getting Here

The park is easily accessible by car, bus, and train. Use our directions guide to find the most convenient route from your location.

Entry Fees

Our entry fees vary depending on the season and visitor age group. Discounts are available for groups and long-term visits. Check our pricing page for more details.

Opening Hours

WildHaven Park is open every day from 8 AM to 6 PM. Special events may affect opening hours, so make sure to check the schedule in advance.

Travel Packages

We offer all-inclusive travel packages that include accommodation, guided tours, meals, and activities. Book your package to make your visit hassle-free.
